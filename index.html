About TSR

A Focused Homebuilder
with a Repeatable Model.

Two Stories Residential is a Los Angeles–based homebuilder specializing in ground-up single-family construction on the Westside. We acquire flat teardown lots, demolish the existing structure, and build architecturally refined two-story homes designed for modern living.

Our approach is built on a simple idea: eliminate complexity. We work exclusively in four neighborhoods we know block by block, use pre-engineered plan sets optimized for each lot, and maintain long-standing relationships with the same trades and consultants on every project. The result is a 12–15 month cycle from acquisition to sale — with predictable quality at every stage.

TSR has completed 19 projects across approximately 82,000 square feet. Every project has been profitable.

How We Work

Lot to Living Space in 12–15 Months

Every project follows the same disciplined cycle — no discretionary approvals, no speculation, no surprises. Fully by-right execution from start to finish.

01

Acquire

1–2 Months

All-cash purchase of a flat teardown lot. We underwrite thoroughly before making an offer — no contingencies, no re-trades.

02

Design & Permit

~2 Months

Pre-engineered plan sets tailored to each lot. Fully by-right — no variances, no design review boards, no discretionary approvals required.

03

Build

~9 Months

Ground-up construction with standardized scopes, value-engineered budgets, recurring trades, and quality control frameworks refined across 19 projects.

04

Sell

1–3 Months

Listed in deeply liquid markets where the majority of comparable homes sell within 30 days. Median days on market: 11–26 across our neighborhoods.
